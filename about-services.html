About The Services

Diving deep into the seven-step meticulous craftsmanship that defines every masterpiece. From the initial charcoal sketch to the grand gallery unveiling, witness the classical journey behind true high-end fine art.

PHASE 01

Concept &
Composition

Every great painting begins with a profound vision. We engage in deep philosophical dialogues to understand the emotion you wish to capture, creating multiple charcoal studies and compositional sketches to ensure the absolute perfect foundation before a single drop of oil touches the canvas.

Inquire Now ↗
PHASE 02

The Classical
Palette

Color is the voice of the painting. Inspired by the Old Masters, we hand-grind the finest historic pigments and mix them with cold-pressed linseed oil. This organic approach allows us to achieve rich, luminous hues and dramatic undertones that commercial paints simply cannot rival.

Explore Colors ↗
PHASE 03

Masterful
Brushwork

The soul of the artwork lives in its texture. We apply dozens of impasto layers and delicate translucent glazes across weeks of careful work. Each brushstroke is intentional, building unparalleled depth and creating a tactile surface that commands attention from every angle in the room.

Commission Art ↗
PHASE 04

The Curing
Process

Unlike modern synthetics, traditional oil paints do not simply dry; they polymerize and cure over incredible spans of time. We place the artwork in a climate-controlled atelier, allowing the heavy impasto layers to properly oxidize. This patient waiting period ensures the absolute structural integrity of the masterpiece.

Learn The Process ↗
PHASE 05

Bespoke 24k
Gilding

For select pieces requiring a touch of the divine, we hand-apply genuine 24-karat gold leaf using a centuries-old water gilding technique. These delicate metallic accents catch ambient light uniquely at every hour of the day, transforming the painting into a living, breathing entity within your space.

View Gold Art ↗
PHASE 06

Archival Varnish
& Framing

To ensure your masterpiece lasts for generations, once fully cured, it is sealed with a non-yellowing museum-grade archival varnish. We then assist you in selecting a custom, hand-carved solid wood frame tailored to both the painting's aura and your specific interior architecture.

Frame Selection ↗
PHASE 07

The Grand
Unveiling

The acquisition of fine art should be celebrated. Upon completion, we coordinate a private, champagne-catered unveiling event directly in your residence or a private gallery space. The artwork is presented under curated temporary lighting before being permanently installed by our white-glove team.
